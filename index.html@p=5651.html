Host / Havas

Brand Idents

Award-winning Australian creative agency Host/Havas approached us to define a motion design system which would push their ‘H’ brand mark into a sophisticated 3D space. We developed a system which sets the framework for multiple idents to be created in future for the global Havas group, within a consistent brand design.

Havas group is a global ‘village’ of agencies in more than 100 different countries, all connected, working together and offering varied and complimentary skillsets. As one agency in the village, Host/Havas Sydney is a gateway for clients to access to the full network of talent. This idea was the seed of the visual concept we developed at BilalAhmad: Multiple layers all coming together to form one greater whole.
